A simple and powerful **Character Counter** app built with **React** + **Vite**. This app allows you to analyze your text in multiple ways and provides detailed insights.

![A character counter app including abality of counting chracters, counting words, counting sentences and counting pages. Also counting the most common use character in your text](https://github.com/farshadarbab2024/character-counter/blob/main/public/screenshot.png)

## Features
- **Count Characters** – Get the total number of characters in your text.  
- **Count Words** – Count the number of words.  
- **Count Sentences** – Analyze how many sentences are in your text.  
- **Count Pages** – Estimate the number of pages your text would take.  
- **Most Common Characters** – Identify the 4 most frequently used characters and see their usage percentages.  
- **Copy & Clear** – Easily copy your text to the clipboard or clear it with one click.  

## Tech Stack
- **React**  
- **TypeScript**
- **TailwindCSS**
